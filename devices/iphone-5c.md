## Apple iPhone 5c

**Year:** 2013  
**OS:** iOS 7 (upgradeable to iOS 8)  
**CPU:** Apple A6  
**RAM:** 1 GB  
**Storage:** 8 / 16 / 32 GB  

The iPhone 5c was Apple's polycarbonate mid-range iPhone, introduced alongside the 5s. It was essentially the internals of the iPhone 5 in a colorful plastic shell.
