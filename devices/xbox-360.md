## Microsoft Xbox 360

**Year:** 2005  
**CPU:** IBM Xenon (3× PowerPC cores @ 3.2 GHz)  
**GPU:** ATI Xenos  
**RAM:** 512 MB GDDR3  
**Storage:** 20 / 60 / 120 / 250 GB HDD (model dependent)  

The Xbox 360 was Microsoft's second home console and dominated the mid-2000s alongside the PS3. Notable for Xbox Live, Achievements, and titles like Halo 3 and Gears of War.
